Among our students are many Europeans, Americans and also some Asian visitors .

  • Courses :
    *Spanish *Quechua *Weaving *Panflute * Flute * Natural Dyes * Peruvian Cooking

  • Adventures Trips to :
    * High Jungle * Low Jungle * Mountain Areas * Cultural & Ethnical Journeys * Documentaries & Research Traveling .

  • Our students are travellers, some professionals and also university students .

  • At our main office we offer services such as : E-mail, fax, telephone, flights reservations / confirmations tourist information, bike rental, book exchange .

  • And also help with contacts for your next destinations as we have many good friends all over PERU .

 

Our Valley has a great reputation for festivals - we have a lot of parties during the year . Of course the best time to come weatherwise is from March to October ( dry season ) . We have the festivals of Pre - Incas & Inca originis as well as Religious Celebrations including a wide variety of Folkloric Dances , Costumes and local food at every party !

We encourage our students to enjoy the parties as they are good for improving their spanish skills.

We also have activities like barbecues, biking , camping, as well as Mountain & Jungle trips .

The Valley is a beautiful peaceful place good for biking & trekking . You can do it by your own or by organized trips .We also have a many local craftsmen in the villages around The Mantaro Valley .

During the rainy season we have quite a lot of rain fall, mainly in the morning or evenings - October to March .

During the rest of the year we have blue skys a blessing for a normaly dry weather .
