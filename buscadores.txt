THE LEARNING & TRAVEL INFORMATION CENTER</title>

Contenido:
INCAS DEL PERU is a comprehensive tourist information and service centre.
In addition to basic travel, we specilize in providing a link to the distinctive
cultures of Peru in the mountains and jungles regions, incas offers both adventure
tourism, which allows visitors to share the lifestyles of the local people, as well
courses in language, artisanal skills and folkloric customs which allow a more
intensive cultural experience.
