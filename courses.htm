We have, for those travellers, looking for extra special activities, the following alternative courses :

a.- Weaving Courses : Traditional backstrap looms from the Pre hispanic times, Pedal loom, with aditional courses on spinning and natural dyes techniques .

b.- Dancing Courses : Many fockloric dances, with experienced dancers with aditional visits to the villages where these dances are performed.

c.- Cooking Courses : Delicious and healthy peruvian dishes, including the fun of shopping at the the colorful local market with exotic indian flavours.

d.- Music Courses : Traditional Andean Music with typical instruments like The Andean Flute, Panflute, or Charango ( small guitar ) and Guitar with a simple numerical system .
