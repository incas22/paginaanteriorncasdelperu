Some Information about our Programs :

Homestay Programs

Homestay is a very important component of our programs.

 
Spanish speaking host families offer a good opportunity to share and enjoy friendly conversations and discussions that will improve your Spanish.

Most of our homes are in towns or in nearby villages- no more than 15 minutes distance to school. Cost for transportation is about $US 0.50 each way and buses run constantly all day.

The are 4 room available in a hotel (La Casa de la Abuela)  and in town we have several families offering rooms. Each student is placed with a separate family. We do not place more than 1 student per family, although friends and couples can be placed in one family if you wish.

  • Lodging includes three meals a day.

  • We have some families that provide homestay with private bathrooms. Most other families have shared toilets with good hygienic conditions .

  • There are many opportunities to experience the culture and lifestyle of a uniquely Peruvian Andean City .

  • Huancayo is not a touristic city, and our people are very welcoming towards the travellers who take the trouble to visit us.
