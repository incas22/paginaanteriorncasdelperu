Homestay
Programs

Homestay is a very important component
of our programs.

 

Spanish speaking host families offer a good opportunity to share and enjoy friendly conversations and discussions that will improve your spanish .

Most of our homes are in towns or in nearby villages- no more than 15 minutes distance to school . Cost for transportation is about $ 0.50 U.S. per way - buses run constantly all day .

The school has 4 rooms available and in town we have several families offering rooms . Each student is placed with one family. Unless required, we do not place more than 1 student per family .

  • Friends and Couples can be placed in one family .

  • Lodging includes three meals a day .

  • We have some families that provide homestay with private toilet. Most other families have shared toilets with good hygienic conditions .

  • There are many opportunities to experience the culture and lifestyle of a uniquely Peruvian Andean City .

  • Huancayo is not a Touristic city, thus our people are very welcoming towards the travellers who visit us .

 

 

 

Language Program

  • Personalized and professional instruction ( 1 teacher - 1 student ) except when students arrive in a group and want to be together. 

  • We have the lessons in the morning or in the afternoon with a short break of 10 minutes during the lessons .

  • Our lessons are given at a beautiful house with gardens and a very quiet atmosphere .

  • Oportunities to form lasting friendship with students and Peruvian families .

  • We organize weekly day time excursions .

  • The levels we offer are :

a) Basic beginners

b) Medium

c) Conversational.

  • In the first two levels we use a text book especially designed for these levels. For the conversational course we use reading materials with a wide range of vocabulary.

  • Our teachers are all qualified teachers, 25- 35 years old and some speak English.
