QUECHUA LESSONS

  • The courses for Quechua follow the same format as the Spanish Lessons .
  • The Quechua language is central to Andean life. To learn it you will need at least two weeks of basic vocabulary and a month to be able to engage in basic conversation.
  • The reward of learning Quechua is that a whole new culture will become available to you.
  • Extra lessons can be taken, at an additional cost of $ US 8 per hour.

 

ADDITIONAL COURSES

We have, for those travellers, looking for extra special activities, the following courses :

a.- Weaving Courses : Learn to use traditional backstrap looms from Pre-hispanic times, or a pedal loom, with additional courses on spinning and how to use natural dyes.

b.- Dancing Courses: Learn many of the local folkloric dances from experienced dancers. Visit the villages where these dances are performed.

c.-Cooking Courses: Cook delicious and healthy Peruvian dishes, including the fun of shopping at the colourful local market and using exotic local ethnic flavours.

d.-Music Courses : Learn to play a variety of intruments such as Andean flute, panflute, charango (small guitar) and guitar. Our methods are simple and practical.

PRICES :

  • A minimum of 15 hours is required to book.

 INCAS DEL PERU . . .

The Adventures . . .

a.- The High Jungle . . . a hidden paradise on the slopes of the Andes, east of Huancayo. A unique experience in a place surrounded by waterfalls, wild vegetation and the mystery of the cloud forest.

We have a hut with basic facilities - no electricity, out in the middle of nowhere.

This very simple hut is my father's home. You will spend a few days in this place, which will be a base for our walks, some climbing - you'll get totally wet and still enjoy it! I promise.

b.- Huaytapallana . . . ..

A visit to this holy mountain, to experience a bit of the life of our Andean people. A variety of options from a one day trek to six days are available. It is also possible to get porters and llamas to carry your packs, in order to enjoy a bit more the beautiful landscape.

c.-Esoteric Journeys . . . A special trip only for very special people in search of meaning and fulfilment. Huaytapallana is a very sacred place, but to channel the good energy of this place we get help from a very special couple. I can testify to the effect on my own life.... Sorry, special conditions apply to take this trip.

d.- Biking Trips . . . You can bike from village to village as we have many trails and back roads, but we also have some trips involving camping and a very special one covering the three geografical regions of Peru over 14 days.

One very popular trip is a one day trek to the mountains with the down hills on full suspension mountain bikes.

The Mantaro Valley by car . . . for those with limited time this option covers a circuit of the craft villages, historical places and the best spots for fun and eating traditional food.

We often go for fun on walks and treks around the Valley - you've just got to be lucky arriving at Casa de la Abuela and join us for a walk, for this walks everyone is welcome paying their own local transportation about $0.50 USD per way.

Party time. . . We have so many parties that often we get our students together with other fellow travellers to go to local fiestas. The party starts at midnight plus one second with fireworks, so we have to get there at the right time.

As you can see from this summary, you might be entering a very special experiance in your life. Peru is full of beautiful places, some very well known, some just for those who want to get off the beaten trail. You are welcome in Huancayo, a place in the heart of Peru, with a vibrant culture and friendly people.
