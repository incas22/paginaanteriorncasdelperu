Welcome to Incas del Peru . . .

We are glad you contacted us, and are pleased to welcome you to INCAS DEL PERU. With this letter we are enclosing our latest information .

Huancayo is located in the Andean mountains at 3240 mts. In altitude. We have lots of inviting trails and roads to walk or bike. We organize walks and try to involve the students in various activities .

The weather is sunny from April to October. The other months are rainy, cloudy, a bit cold , but there are still dry days.

The prices listed are to be paid in advance, that is, before we start the course. The levels we offer for our students are : Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced-Conversational courses .

We hope our information is clear and complete , but please contact us if you have further questions. You may do so by Fax or E-mail .
